About

Rewild is a web browser game made to explore the relationship between interactivity and artwork.

Rewild is an artistic piece which aims to emphasize choices. It forces the viewer to make the choice to continually examine the story and art, rather than simply displaying the work. Divya created this artwork through encoding the website and creating all of the artwork over the span of two semesters.
